# 25-Day Sprint Plan: Foundation & Activation

**Version:** 1.0
**Date:** 27 January 2026
**Status:** Active
**Classification:** Internal Operations

---

## Objective

To establish the foundational rhythm of Season 2 by activating the Board, initiating critical workstreams, and securing early wins. This sprint is about translating strategy into immediate, tangible action.

---

## Week 1: Foundation & Activation (Days 1-7)

**Goal:** Activate the Board, establish operational rhythm, and launch public presence.

| Day | Owner | Action | KISS Stack | AI Assistance |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1-2 | Board | **Board Kickoff Meeting:** Ratify 25/90-day plan, confirm roles, establish decision log protocol. | Notion (Agenda), Repo (Minutes) | Draft meeting agenda, summarize key decisions for the log. |

## Success Criteria for 25-Day Sprint

1.  Board is meeting regularly with a documented decision log.
2.  Public website is live.
3.  At least one grant application has been submitted.
4.  Legal entity setup is in progress.
5.  The 90-day strategic plan is finalized and ratified.
