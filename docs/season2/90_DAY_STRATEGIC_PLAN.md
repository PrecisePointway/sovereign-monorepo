## AI-Assisted Operations Framework

The Sovereign Agent will be deployed across all workstreams to assist with:

- **Research & Analysis:** Continuously scan for new grant opportunities, regulatory changes, and potential partners.
- **Document Drafting:** Generate first drafts of grant applications, reports, case studies, and legal documents.
- **Project Management:** Track progress against the 25/90-day plans, send reminders, and flag blockers.
- **Communication:** Draft internal and external communications, including emails, blog posts, and social media updates.

**The AI is a coordinator and a calculator, not a governor.** All final decisions are made by the Board.
