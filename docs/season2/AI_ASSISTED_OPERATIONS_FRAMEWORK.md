# AI-Assisted Operations Framework

**Version:** 1.0
**Date:** 27 January 2026
**Status:** Active
**Classification:** Internal Operations

---

## 1.0 Principle: The AI is a Coordinator, Not a Governor

This framework outlines how the Sovereign Agent (the AI) will be deployed to assist with Season 2 operations. The core principle is immutable:

> **The AI assists, advises, and automates. It does not decide, command, or govern.**

All final decisions are made by the Board. The AI's role is to augment human intelligence, not replace it. It is a tool for efficiency, not a source of authority.

## 3.0 The Human-in-the-Loop Protocol

Every action taken by the AI must be auditable and approved by a human operator. We will enforce a strict Human-in-the-Loop (HITL) protocol for all critical operations.

### 3.1 Tiers of AI Action

| Tier | Action Type | HITL Requirement |
|---|---|---|
| **Tier 1: Automated Assistance** | - Research<br>- Summarization<br>- Data analysis | **Passive Review:** AI actions are logged and reviewed periodically by the relevant team member. |
| **Tier 2: Draft Generation** | - Drafting emails<br>- Drafting documents<br>- Filling forms | **Active Review & Approval:** All generated drafts must be reviewed, edited, and approved by a human before being sent or submitted. |
| **Tier 3: External Interaction** | - Sending emails<br>- Posting to social media<br>- Submitting applications | **STRICTLY PROHIBITED:** The AI is not authorized to interact with external systems or individuals on behalf of the organization. All external communication is conducted by human team members. |

### 3.2 The Evidence Chain

Every significant action taken by the AI will be recorded in a cryptographically secured evidence chain, consistent with the principles of the Sovereign Governance System. This ensures a complete and auditable record of all AI-assisted operations.

## 4.0 Technical Implementation

- **Sovereign Agent:** The core AI will be the `sovereign-agent` developed in Season 1, running on our own infrastructure to ensure data sovereignty.
- **Tool Integration:** The agent will interact with the KISS stack tools via their APIs where available, or through browser automation for tools without APIs.
- **Local First:** All data and models will be stored and processed locally wherever possible. Cloud services will be used only when strictly necessary and with a clear data-sovereignty strategy.

---

## 5.0 The Goal: Augmented Efficiency, Not Autonomous Operation

The purpose of this framework is to make our human team more efficient, not to build a fully autonomous organization. By automating the mundane and time-consuming aspects of our work, we free up our team to focus on what they do best: strategy, creativity, and building relationships.
