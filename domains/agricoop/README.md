# AgriCoop

**A cooperative operating system that removes intermediaries, not agency.**

---

## The Canonical Line

> **AgriCoop does not automate farming. It automates coordination, verification, and logistics so farmers can farm.**

---

## What AgriCoop Is

- **Surfaces options**
- **Coordinates logistics**
- **Records evidence**
- **Reduces admin and friction**
- **Executes decisions humans explicitly make**

## What AgriCoop Is NOT

- Does NOT decide what to plant
- Does NOT decide who is trustworthy
- Does NOT decide prices
- Does NOT optimise people

**The AI is a clerk, coordinator, and calculator — not a governor.**

---

## Core Modules

1. **Coordination Hub** — Member management, voting, scheduling
2. **Contribution Ledger** — Evidence of deliveries and compliance
3. **Order Aggregator** — Consumer demand visibility
