Risk Profile:
Survivable Failures, Real Optionality

If Adoption Is Low
The cost is sunk development time, not ongoing liability. The free model means no refunds, no broken contracts, and no churn risk.
Impact: Contained
If Components Fail
The system is modular. A failing part (like an alert router) can be isolated without bringing down the core evidence engine.
Impact: Isolated
If Reputation Hits
Because the core is free and open, there is no "scam" narrative. The worst case is "it didn't work," not "they took our money."
Impact: Defensible
Season 3 Is Optional
Paid services are demand-driven. There is no commitment to build until a buyer asks.
